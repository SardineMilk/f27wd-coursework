The expenses associated with our services, our compensation, and commission

As a licensed organization under the Financial Conduct Authority (FCA), our revenue is solely derived from commission and additional fees, such as our arrangement fee. The arrangement fee is associated with our services and is applied at the time of placing an order. This fee is designed to cover the expenses incurred by LINGsCARS.com, including facilitating the entire process from order placement to coordinating the delivery of your vehicle.

Upon ordering a vehicle, you will enter into a lease agreement with one of our partner finance providers/lenders. We receive commission from the finance provider/lender once the agreement becomes active. While the commission may be pre-set, it can vary and will impact the amount payable by the customer. It's important to note that LINGsCARS.com operates as a broker, not a lender, and collaborates with multiple suppliers and/or funders.
